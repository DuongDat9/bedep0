About

Many animals around the world are facing extinction due to habitat loss, climate change, and illegal hunting. Protecting these species is essential to maintaining Earth’s biodiversity.

Quick Facts

  • Over 40,000 species are listed in the IUCN Red List.
  • Habitat destruction is the main cause of extinction.
  • Conservation programs help protect endangered species.

Famous Endangered Animals

  • Hawksbill Sea Turtle

Most Endangered Species by Region
  1. Asia: Sumatran Orangutan, Pangolin
  2. Africa: Black Rhino, African Forest Elephant
  3. South America: Amazon River Dolphin, Giant Otter
  4. Australia: Koala, Northern Hairy-nosed Wombat
  5. Europe: European Mink, Iberian Lynx
